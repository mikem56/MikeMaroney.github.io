Covid Data Exploration

In this project, I analyzed COVID data from the start of the pandemic to the present. I looked at infection and death rates across countries compared to their populations. I was able to explore how different geographic regions were affected by the pandemic, and using calculations I was able to look at rolling data on vaccinations rates. Check out the queries I used!
